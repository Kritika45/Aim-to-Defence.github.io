UPSC NDA Exam

NDA is conducted by the Union Public Service Commission (UPSC) twice a year. This national level exam is held to recruit the candidates for the Army, Navy and Air Force wings of the Indian Naval Academy Course (INAC) and National Defence Academy (NDA).The UPSC is solely responsible for issuing guidelines for selection.

CDS/CDS OTA Exam

CDS Exam (Combined Defence Services) Exam is scheduled twice a year by UPSC. Candidates should fulfill different parameters of eligibility criteria such as nationality, educational qualification,age, marital status, and physical conditions. Candidates appearing for the CDS 2022 exam must have completed their graduation or at least be appearing for the final exam of the degree.

Indian Coast Guard

The Indian Coast Guard is a maritime law enforcement and search and rescue agency of India with jurisdiction over its territorial waters including its contiguous zone and exclusive economic zone. The Indian Coast Guard was formally established on 1 February 1977 by the Coast Guard Act, 1978 of the Parliament of India.

UPSC NDA 2022(Girls and Boys)

The NDA 2022 official notification has been released on the official website on December 22, 2021. The NDA 1 application forms are live and the candidates can submit them by January 11, 2022. The candidates must check the NDA eligibility criteria before applying for the exam.

CDS/CDS OTA 2022

UPSC has released the CDS exam dates 2022 for the CDS I & CDS II exam along with the UPSC Calendar 2022. As per the notification the CDS I 2022 Exam is scheduled to be held on 10th April 2022 & CDS II 2022 on 04th September 2022.

AFCAT 2022

AFCAT 1 2022 exam is scheduled from February 12-14, 2022. The admit card has been released on the official website. The details about the exam centre and timings are given on the admit card. The appearing candidates must practice previous year question paper for better preparation.
